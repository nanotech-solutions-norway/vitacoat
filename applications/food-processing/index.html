Food Processing Applications

Persistent Hygiene Support for Non-Food-Contact Hard Surfaces

Control panels, equipment housings, stainless environmental surfaces, refrigerator handles, and shared operator touchpoints face continuous contamination pressure between scheduled sanitation events. VitaCoat is positioned for food-area surface hygiene support on non-food-contact hard surfaces.

Typical Food-Area Application Zones

Control Panels

High-touch HMI and control surfaces where operators interact continuously throughout production shifts.

Equipment Housings

Non-product-contact hard surfaces surrounding core equipment in humid and cleaning-intensive environments.

Shared Touchpoints

Refrigerator handles, access points, stainless work-area surrounds, and operator-interface surfaces.

Compatible Surface Categories

  • Grade 304 and 316 stainless steel
  • Powder-coated and coated steel panels
  • Anodized aluminum structural elements
  • ABS and engineering-plastic housings

Important Boundary

VitaCoat is positioned for food-area environmental surfaces. It is not presented as certified for direct food contact. Suitability for specific food-contact-adjacent use must be reviewed by your technical and regulatory teams.

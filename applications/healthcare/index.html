Healthcare Applications

Persistent Surface Protection for Clinical High-Touch Zones

Patient rooms, nurse stations, bed rails, IV poles, door hardware, carts, and shared device surfaces face continuous microbial pressure. VitaCoat is positioned to supplement infection-control cleaning programs by maintaining a transparent hygienic layer between scheduled disinfection cycles.

Where VitaCoat Fits in Healthcare

Bed Rails & Patient Furniture

High-frequency patient and staff contact surfaces where recontamination begins quickly after routine cleaning.

Door Handles & Hardware

Transition points touched by patients, visitors, clinicians, and support staff throughout each shift.

Nurse Stations & Shared Workspaces

Countertops, keyboards, trays, and shared peripherals in dense multi-user environments.

Typical Compatible Surface Categories

  • Powder-coated steel
  • Anodized aluminum
  • Medical-grade ABS and engineering plastics
  • Treated glass and display surfaces

Important Boundary

VitaCoat supplements routine cleaning and disinfection protocols. It is not positioned as a replacement for infection-prevention procedures, terminal cleaning, or regulated hygiene controls.

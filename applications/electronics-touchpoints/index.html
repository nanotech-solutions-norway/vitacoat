Electronics & Touchpoints

Persistent Protection for Shared Device Surfaces

Kiosks, ATMs, check-in screens, payment terminals, and shared peripherals are among the highest-contact surfaces in any public or institutional environment. VitaCoat is positioned to provide a transparent, non-corrosive, non-conductive supplementary hygienic layer on these hard surfaces.

Primary Device Categories

Self-Service Kiosks

Retail, transit, and service terminals with continuous public use.

ATMs & Payment Terminals

Pin pads, card-reader surrounds, and display surfaces under very high contact density.

Check-In Screens

Healthcare, travel, and hospitality environments with rapidly rotating users.

Shared Peripherals

Keyboards, mice, library terminals, and other communal computing equipment.

Material Compatibility Focus

Validated positioning centers on glass, treated displays, engineering plastics, and non-conductive device housings.

Important Boundary

VitaCoat is positioned for external device surfaces and housings. It is not intended for direct application to exposed circuitry, PCB assemblies, or connector interfaces.

Applications

Matching the Right Surface Environment to the Right Protection

VitaCoat is a persistent antimicrobial barrier engineered for high-touch, hard, non-porous surfaces across demanding environments. Its SiO₂-bound citrus bioflavonoid matrix is positioned to deliver up to six months of continuous hygienic protection while supplementing existing cleaning protocols rather than disrupting them.

Application Selection

One Formulation. Multiple Critical Environments.

VitaCoat is not a broad-spectrum disinfectant. It is a durable, surface-bonded antimicrobial coating designed to maintain hygienic integrity on hard non-porous substrates between scheduled cleaning intervals. The five application clusters below represent environments where interval contamination risk is highest, cleaning frequency is structurally constrained, and surface hygiene directly affects outcomes.

How to use this guide

Each application cluster links to a dedicated technical brief covering substrate compatibility, application context, and maintenance expectations. If your environment spans multiple clusters, the technical review process can be used to develop a unified specification.

VitaCoat supplements routine cleaning. It does not replace disinfection protocols or satisfy regulatory decontamination requirements on its own.

Persistent Protection

Why the Interval Between Cleanings Is Where Risk Accumulates

Cleaning and disinfection events provide a momentary reset of surface bioburden. Re-contamination begins almost immediately after surfaces return to service. Rather than depending on increased cleaning frequency, VitaCoat is positioned to establish a durable antimicrobial layer at the surface level that remains active through the interval between cleaning events.

Surface Compatibility

Hard-Surface Compatibility — What VitaCoat Bonds To

Glass & Treated Displays

Standard glass, tempered glass, anti-glare treated panels, and compatible display surfaces.

Stainless & Powder-Coated Steel

Grade 304/316 stainless, powder-coated panels, and galvanized hardware.

Anodized Aluminum & Engineering Plastics

Architectural aluminum, ABS, polycarbonate, HDPE, and hard equipment housings.

VitaCoat is engineered for hard, non-porous surfaces. It is not suitable for textile, porous stone, unfinished wood, untreated concrete, or surfaces intended for direct food contact without separate validation.

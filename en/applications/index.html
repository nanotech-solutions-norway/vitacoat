VitaCoat
Applications

The right surface environment needs the right protection model

VitaCoat is developed as a persistent antimicrobial barrier for hard, non-porous high-touch surfaces in demanding environments. The SiO₂-bound bioflavonoid technology supports hygiene control between routine cleaning cycles.

Application selection

One formulation. Multiple critical environments.

VitaCoat is not a broad-spectrum disinfectant. It is a durable, surface-bonded antimicrobial coating developed to support hygienic integrity on hard, non-porous substrates between planned cleaning intervals.

How to use this overview

VitaCoat supplements routine cleaning. It does not replace disinfection protocols or regulated decontamination requirements.

Persistent protection

Why risk builds between cleaning intervals

Cleaning and disinfection provide a temporary reduction in surface burden. Recontamination often starts immediately once the surface is put back into use. VitaCoat is designed to establish a persistent functional layer between intervals.

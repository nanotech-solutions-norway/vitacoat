Document categories

What the documentation package should cover

Technical reports

Performance, durability, substrate fit, test basis, and project-specific technical review.

Standards and testing

EN-, ISO-, JIS-, and ASTM-oriented material where relevant to the evaluation area.

Safety and environment

SDS, EHS-relevant positioning, chemistry profile, and claim boundaries.

Application

Pretreatment, application, curing, quality control, and operational handover.

Maintenance

Cleaning compatibility, inspection, service interval, and reapplication.

Decision workflow

How documentation supports technical due diligence

1. Screen scope

Define use case, substrate, environment, cleaning regime, and documentation requirements.

2. Match documents

Connect the right document pack to standards, safety, application, and maintenance.

3. Validate fit

Review compatibility, claim boundaries, pilot needs, and internal approval logic.

4. Decide next step

Proceed to technical review, pilot, procurement process, or limited document review.

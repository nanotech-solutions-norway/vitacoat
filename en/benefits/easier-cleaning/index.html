Benefit

Easier surface maintenance through hydrophobic anti-adhesive behavior

VitaCoat is positioned not only as a persistent hygienic barrier layer, but also as a surface treatment that changes how liquids, residues, and soils behave on the substrate. The result is intended to support easier routine cleaning and faster return to service.

Reduced soil adhesion

Residues are positioned to bind less strongly to the treated surface.

Lower cleaning effort

Routine wipe-downs may require less mechanical effort on compatible treated surfaces.

Faster drying behavior

Hydrophobic surface behavior can support faster runoff and shorter residual moisture presence.

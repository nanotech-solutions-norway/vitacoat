Application Process

Four operational steps from preparation to return to service

This page organizes the VitaCoat application workflow into a clear sequence for project review, installer planning, and internal rollout preparation.

Request Technical ReviewOpen Documentation

1. Prepare the surface

Clean and degrease thoroughly. Remove residues, incompatible contaminants, and anything that may weaken adhesion.

2. Apply evenly

Apply a thin, even layer using the approved wipe-on method and maintain consistent coverage across the full target surface.

3. Respect cure time

Allow the required cure interval before returning the surface to full operational use.

4. Confirm early result

Verify finish quality, operating conditions, and maintenance handover before routine use resumes.

Safety & Environment

Built for technical, EHS, and procurement review

VitaCoat safety and environment positioning is built around a formulation without heavy-metal antimicrobial systems, low-emission narrative, and the use of naturally derived citrus bioflavonoid chemistry in a durable SiO₂ matrix.

Open DocumentationRequest Technical Review

RoHS / REACH positioning

Supports electronics-facing, procurement-facing, and EHS-facing review where regulated-substance control matters.

VOC-free / low-odor narrative

Relevant for occupied environments such as healthcare, offices, public buildings, and educational facilities.

No heavy-metal antimicrobial system

Differentiates VitaCoat from silver- and copper-based antimicrobial chemistry models.

Hypoallergenic / non-irritant positioning

Supports safety review in environments with routine human contact.

Naturally derived citrus bioflavonoids

The active chemistry is positioned as naturally derived rather than primarily synthetic biocidal chemistry.

Lifecycle efficiency

The multi-month service interval supports a more resource-conscious positioning through lower reapplication frequency.

All safety, compatibility, and environment positioning should be read together with the full technical documentation and any project-specific review requirements before final specification or procurement decisions are made.

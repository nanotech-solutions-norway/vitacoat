Durability

Durability Framing for Real-World High-Touch Service

VitaCoat’s durability narrative is built around accelerated touch simulation, retained hydrophobic character, and an expected multi-month service interval under normal-use conditions. This page consolidates those proof points for technical review.

What Supports Longevity

  • Correct surface preparation
  • Proper application and curing
  • Suitable substrate compatibility
  • Non-abrasive maintenance approach

What Reduces Service Life

  • Abrasive cleaning methods
  • Harsh chemical exposure
  • High-friction contact surfaces
  • Unsuitable substrates or misuse
Actual durability varies by substrate, contact intensity, cleaning chemistry, and the operating environment. Project-specific review remains necessary before deployment commitments are made.

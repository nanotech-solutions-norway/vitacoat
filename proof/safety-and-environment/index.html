Safety & Environment

Built for Procurement, EHS, and Occupied-Environment Review

VitaCoat’s safety and environmental narrative centers on a non-heavy-metal formulation approach, low-emission positioning, and the use of natural citrus bioflavonoid chemistry in a durable SiO₂ matrix.

Open DocumentationRequest Technical Review

RoHS / REACH Positioning

Supports electronics, procurement, and EHS review workflows where regulated-substance control is relevant.

VOC-Free / Odorless Narrative

Supports occupied-environment use cases such as healthcare, offices, public facilities, and education spaces.

No Heavy-Metal Antimicrobials

Positions VitaCoat apart from silver- and copper-based antimicrobial chemistry systems.

Hypo-Allergenic / Non-Irritant Framing

Supports safety review for environments with routine human contact.

Natural Citrus Bioflavonoids

Frames the active chemistry as naturally derived rather than primarily synthetic biocide chemistry.

Lifecycle Efficiency

The multi-month service interval supports an eco-conscious positioning through lower repeat-application frequency.

All safety, compatibility, and environmental positioning should be read together with the full technical documentation and any project-specific review requirements before final specification or procurement decisions are made.

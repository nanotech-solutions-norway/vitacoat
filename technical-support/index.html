Inquiry Inputs

What Information to Include in Your Inquiry

Application Environment

Temperature range, humidity, UV exposure, chemical-contact likelihood, and whether the environment is interior, exterior, or transitional.

Substrate Type

Material, grade, finish, and prior treatment history. Surface energy, porosity, and contamination history directly affect system selection.

Cleaning Regime

Cleaning chemistry, frequency, and method, including any regulatory hygiene standards that must be maintained post-application.

Project Scale & Timeline

Total surface area, number of zones, phasing approach, design freeze, procurement milestone, and intended application window.

Complete submissions route faster. Incomplete inquiries are not rejected, but they require clarification before full technical assessment can begin.

Surface Compatibility

Designed for Hard, Non-Porous Surface Environments

This page clarifies where VitaCoat fits best from a substrate perspective and where additional caution, sampling, or project-specific review is required before deployment.

Request Technical ReviewOpen Documentation

Best-fit surfaces

  • Glass
  • Metal
  • Engineered plastics
  • Laminates and other hard, non-porous finishes

Requires review

  • Specialty coatings
  • Unknown mixed-material assemblies
  • High-friction interfaces
  • Highly sensitive finishes

Not a lead-use category

  • High-abrasion flooring-first use
  • Direct-food-contact assumptions without separate evidence
  • Any substrate not supported by technical review
Compatibility should be confirmed on representative substrate samples before scale deployment, especially where finish sensitivity, friction, chemical exposure, or electronics adjacency are part of the operating context.
